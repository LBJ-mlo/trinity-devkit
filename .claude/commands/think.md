Invoke the `comprehensive-thinking` skill.

Take `$ARGUMENTS` as the question or topic to analyze deeply. If `$ARGUMENTS` is empty, ask the user what they want to think through comprehensively.

The comprehensive-thinking skill applies a five-review system:
1. **Define key problems and domains** — surface user's deeper goals
2. **Master theory systems** — bring in relevant theoretical frameworks
3. **Key facts and synthesis** — collect and synthesize evidence
4. **Adversarial pressure testing** — strongest counter-arguments
5. **Holistic understanding and verifiable closure** — boundaries + verification path

Triggers automatically for: 全面思考, 深度理解, 系统性分析, 帮我想清楚, 从根上看, 复杂问题拆解, 架构判断, 策略判断, 根因分析
